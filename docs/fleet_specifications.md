## Utilization Targets

| Metric | Minimum | Optimal | Maximum |
|--------|---------|---------|---------|
| Load Factor | 65% | 80-85% | 95% |
| Daily Flight Hours | 8 hrs | 12 hrs | 16 hrs |
| Turnaround Time | - | 2 hrs | 3 hrs |

### Load Factor Guidelines

- **Below 65%**: Route is underperforming. Consider consolidation or schedule adjustment.
- **65-85%**: Optimal operating range. Maintains profitability with buffer for demand spikes.
- **85-95%**: High utilization. Monitor closely for capacity constraints.
- **Above 95%**: Critical. Requires immediate capacity expansion or overflow handling.

## Maintenance Schedule

- **Daily Check**: 30 minutes pre-flight
- **Weekly Check**: 4 hours (rotated across fleet)
- **Monthly Check**: 24 hours per aircraft
- **Annual Overhaul**: 2 weeks per aircraft (staggered)

## Emergency Capacity

In case of demand surge:
- 1 additional 767-300F available from partner airline (48hr notice)
- Charter options available through Zava Charter Partners
- Cross-route aircraft reallocation possible with 24hr notice
