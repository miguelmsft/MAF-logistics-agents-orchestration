# Zava Logistics Route Network

## Overview

Zava Logistics operates four primary cargo routes connecting major US metropolitan areas. Our hub-and-spoke model prioritizes high-density corridors with expansion capability.

## Primary Routes

| Route | Origin | Destination | Distance | Flight Time | Current Frequency |
|-------|--------|-------------|----------|-------------|-------------------|
| LAX-JFK | Los Angeles (LAX) | New York (JFK) | 3,970 km | 5.5 hours | 2x daily |
| ORD-MIA | Chicago (ORD) | Miami (MIA) | 1,910 km | 3.0 hours | 1x daily |
| SEA-DFW | Seattle (SEA) | Dallas (DFW) | 2,700 km | 4.0 hours | 1x daily |
| DEN-ATL | Denver (DEN) | Atlanta (ATL) | 2,035 km | 3.2 hours | 1x daily |

## Route Details

### LAX-JFK (Flagship Route)

- **Hub Type**: Major Hub
- **Aircraft**: Boeing 767-300F
- **Schedule**:
  - Flight 1: Depart LAX 22:00, Arrive JFK 06:30+1
  - Flight 2: Depart LAX 02:00, Arrive JFK 10:30
- **Daily Capacity**: 104,000 kg (2 flights x 52,000 kg)
- **Avg Package Weight**: 3.6 kg
- **Strategic Importance**: Highest revenue route, connects two largest US markets

## Expansion Options

### Short-Term (48 hours - 1 week notice)

| Route | Expansion Option | Lead Time | Cost Impact |
|-------|------------------|-----------|-------------|
| LAX-JFK | Add 3rd daily flight | 48 hours | +15% per kg |
| ORD-MIA | Upgrade to 2x daily | 1 week | +10% per kg |
| SEA-DFW | Add weekend flight | 72 hours | +12% per kg |
| DEN-ATL | Upgrade to 2x daily | 1 week | +10% per kg |

### Charter Options

All routes have charter flight availability:
- **Cost**: 1.5x standard rate
- **Lead Time**: 24-48 hours
- **Provider**: Zava Charter Partners
- **Capacity**: Full aircraft (47,000-52,000 kg)

## Seasonal Considerations

- **Q4 Holiday Season**: All routes may need temporary expansion
- **Valentine's Day (Feb 14)**: LAX-JFK and ORD-MIA see 25-35% surge
- **Summer Months**: Generally lower volume, good for maintenance windows
